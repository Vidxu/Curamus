Types of Therapy Sessions

Aromatherapy Sessions

Aromatherapy involves the use of essential oils to promote physical and psychological well-being. In aromatherapy sessions, therapists utilize the natural scents of essential oils to alleviate stress, enhance mood, and induce relaxation.

Essential oils are often diffused into the air or applied topically through massage, allowing clients to experience their therapeutic benefits.

Group Therapy Sessions

Group therapy involves a small group of individuals coming together to discuss shared experiences, challenges, and emotions under the guidance of a trained therapist. These sessions provide a supportive environment for participants to gain insight, receive feedback, and learn from one another.

Group therapy can be particularly beneficial for individuals struggling with issues such as addiction, trauma, or interpersonal relationships.

Pet Therapy Sessions

Pet therapy, also known as animal-assisted therapy, involves interactions between individuals and trained animals to improve their physical, emotional, and social well-being. In pet therapy sessions, clients interact with therapy animals, such as dogs or horses, under the supervision of a licensed therapist.

Interacting with animals can help reduce stress, alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression, and improve overall mood.
