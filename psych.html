Curamus Psychiatrists

Dr. Emily Johnson

Specialization: Mood Disorders

Dr. Emily Johnson is an experienced psychiatrist with expertise in treating mood disorders such as depression, bipolar disorder, and seasonal affective disorder (SAD). She is dedicated to providing compassionate care and evidence-based treatments to help individuals achieve emotional stability and improved quality of life.

Dr. Michael Smith

Specialization: Anxiety Disorders

Dr. Michael Smith specializes in the treatment of anxiety disorders, including generalized anxiety disorder (GAD), panic disorder, social anxiety disorder, and phobias. He utilizes a combination of cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), medication management, and relaxation techniques to help individuals overcome their fears and regain control of their lives.

Dr. Sarah Adams

Specialization: Trauma and PTSD

Dr. Sarah Adams specializes in treating trauma-related disorders such as post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), complex PTSD, and adjustment disorders. She employs trauma-focused therapies, including eye movement desensitization and reprocessing (EMDR) and prolonged exposure therapy, to help individuals process traumatic experiences and regain a sense of safety and control.

Dr. James Wilson

Specialization: Substance Use Disorders

Dr. James Wilson specializes in the treatment of substance use disorders, including alcoholism, drug addiction, and prescription drug abuse. He adopts a comprehensive approach that combines pharmacotherapy, psychotherapy, and relapse prevention strategies to support individuals in their journey towards sobriety and long-term recovery.

Dr. Jennifer Lee

Specialization: Eating Disorders

Dr. Jennifer Lee specializes in the treatment of eating disorders such as anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa, and binge eating disorder. She employs a multidisciplinary approach that addresses the psychological, nutritional, and medical aspects of eating disorders to help individuals achieve physical and emotional healing.
